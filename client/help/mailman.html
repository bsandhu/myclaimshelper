HOW-TO Submit Claim entries via e-mail

Emails you receive related to a claim can be entered into the system directly via email. Simply copy or forward an email to put@mailman.logicubesolutions.com, making sure that the claim id to which this entry belongs is clearly indicated in the subject. e.g., claim id: CN-123-456

For example, from your gmail, send an email like so:

  1. Send/copy to put@mailman.logicubesolutions.com
  2. Add claim id: 123 to the subject line, where 123 is the Claim ID to which the entry belongs.
  3. Optionally, add any number or hash (#) prefixed #tags to the body of the message.
  4. Submit!
