Can I submit new Tasks (Entries) for a Claim via e-mail?

Yes!
Emails you receive related to a claim can be entered into the system directly from your email client.
Simply forward the email to put@mailman.logicubesolutions.com and include the claim id in the Subject

For example, from your gmail, send an email like so:

  1. To: put@mailman.logicubesolutions.com
  2. Subject: Email subject claim id: X1000
  3. Optionally, add any number or hash (#) prefixed #tags to the body of the message.
  4. Send mail!
