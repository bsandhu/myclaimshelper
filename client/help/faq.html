Can I submit new Tasks (Entries) for a Claim via e-mail?

Yes! Emails you receive related to a claim can be entered into the system directly from your email client. Simply copy or forward an email to put@mailman.logicubesolutions.com, and include the claim id to link the email to in the subject. e.g., claim id: CN-123-456

For example, from your gmail, send an email like so:

  1. To: put@mailman.logicubesolutions.com
  2. Subject: Email subject claim id: 123
  3. Optionally, add any number or hash (#) prefixed #tags to the body of the message.
  4. Submit!
